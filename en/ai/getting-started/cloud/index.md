## Free Use of Xpert AI Cloud Platform

Users can directly access the Xpert AI cloud platform to experience our services for free. After registration, you will receive a certain amount of AI model tokens to call and test our AI models. In this way, you can explore and utilize the powerful features provided by Xpert AI without incurring any costs.

To get started, please visit our [Xpert AI Cloud Platform](https://app.xpertai.cn) and follow the registration process to create your account. Once registered, you will automatically receive an initial quota of AI model tokens. Enjoy our services to the fullest!
